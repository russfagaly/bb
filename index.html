* ERA qualified pitchers must have pitched at least 6 innings (18 outs)
* Little League ERA calculated as (Earned Runs × 6) ÷ Innings Pitched
* Complete Games require 18+ outs pitched in a single game (6 full innings)
* Strike % = (Strikes ÷ Total Pitches) × 100
